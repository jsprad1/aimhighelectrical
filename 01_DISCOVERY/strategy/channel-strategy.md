# Aim High Electrical Services
## Channel Strategy

---

## Strategy Overview

Focus marketing effort and budget on channels that reach Aurora homeowners at the moment they need an electrician. Prioritize high-intent channels (search, local) over awareness channels (social, display).

**Total Recommended Monthly Budget: $2,000-$3,000**

---

## Primary Channels (70% of effort/budget)

### 1. Google Local Services Ads — 30% of budget ($600-$900/mo)

**Why:** Highest intent — homeowners actively searching for an electrician right now. "Google Guaranteed" badge builds instant trust. Pay per lead, not per click.

**Strategy:**
- Optimize Google Business Profile (foundation for LSA)
- Maintain 4.8+ rating with active review management
- Set service area: Aurora, Centennial, Parker, Highlands Ranch
- Highlight: Licensed, insured, veteran-owned

**Content:** Profile optimization, review responses, service descriptions

**KPIs:**
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Cost per lead | $25-$50 |
| Leads per month | 15-25 |
| Lead-to-customer rate | 40-50% |

---

### 2. Google Search Ads — 25% of budget ($500-$750/mo)

**Why:** Captures specific service searches. Complements LSA for broader keyword coverage. Drives to landing page.

**Strategy:**
- 4 campaign groups: General Electrical, Panel Upgrades, EV Chargers, Emergency
- Geo-target: 20-mile radius from Aurora 80016
- Negative keywords: commercial, apprentice, jobs, DIY
- Landing page: https://aimhighelectrical.vercel.app

**Campaign Structure:**

| Ad Group | Sample Keywords | Monthly Budget |
|----------|----------------|----------------|
| General Electrical | electrician aurora co, electrical contractor near me | $150-$250 |
| Panel Upgrades | panel upgrade aurora, electrical panel replacement | $125-$200 |
| EV Chargers | ev charger installation aurora, home ev charger | $100-$150 |
| Emergency/Repair | emergency electrician aurora, electrical repair near me | $125-$150 |

**KPIs:**
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Click-through rate | 4-6% |
| Cost per click | $5-$10 |
| Conversion rate | 8-12% |
| Cost per lead | $40-$75 |

---

### 3. Nextdoor — 15% of budget ($200-$400/mo + organic)

**Why:** Aurora homeowners use Nextdoor heavily for contractor recommendations. Hyper-local trust. Organic engagement is powerful here.

**Strategy:**
- Claim business page with all services listed
- Post helpful tips 2-3x/week (organic)
- Respond to "looking for electrician" requests immediately
- Run targeted local deals and sponsored posts
- Ask satisfied customers to recommend on Nextdoor

**Content Calendar:**
| Day | Content Type |
|-----|-------------|
| Monday | Electrical safety tip |
| Wednesday | Project showcase (before/after) |
| Friday | Seasonal reminder or community post |

**KPIs:**
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Recommendations received | 5-10/month |
| Engagement rate | 3-5% |
| Leads from Nextdoor | 5-10/month |

---

## Secondary Channels (20% of effort/budget)

### 4. Facebook / Instagram — 10% of budget ($200-$300/mo)

**Why:** Brand awareness, project showcases, veteran story amplification. Reaches Personas 1 and 3. Instagram for visual before/after content.

**Strategy:**
- Post 3-4x/week (mix of tips, projects, veteran story, testimonials)
- Run retargeting ads to website visitors
- Join and participate in Aurora community Facebook groups
- Instagram focus on visual project work

**Content Mix:**
| Type | Frequency | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Educational tips | 1x/week | "5 Signs Your Panel Needs Upgrading" |
| Project showcases | 1x/week | Before/after panel upgrade photos |
| Brand story | 1x/week | Veteran story, team spotlight, community involvement |
| Promotional | 1x/week | Seasonal offer, service highlight, CTA |

**KPIs:**
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Page followers | +50/month |
| Post engagement rate | 2-4% |
| Website clicks | 30-50/month |

---

### 5. Email Marketing — 5% of budget ($50/mo platform)

**Why:** Nurture leads, stay top-of-mind with past customers, drive seasonal repeat business. Lowest cost per conversion of any channel.

**Strategy:**
- Welcome sequence for new leads (3 emails)
- Monthly newsletter (seasonal tips + promotion)
- Post-service follow-up (review request + referral ask)
- Seasonal campaigns (storm prep, spring inspection, holiday lighting)

**Content Calendar:**
| Month | Theme | CTA |
|-------|-------|-----|
| Jan-Feb | Winter storm prep, generator promo | Schedule consultation |
| Mar-Apr | Spring safety inspection, panel checkup | Book inspection |
| May-Jun | EV charger season, outdoor lighting | Get estimate |
| Jul-Aug | Home automation, ceiling fans, surge protection | Schedule service |
| Sep-Oct | Basement finishing season, holiday lighting prep | Request quote |
| Nov-Dec | Generator backup, winter weatherization | Book before winter |

**KPIs:**
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| List size growth | +20-30 contacts/month |
| Open rate | 25-35% |
| Click rate | 3-5% |
| Repeat customer bookings | 2-5/month |

---

### 6. Local Partnerships — 5% (time investment)

**Why:** Referrals from complementary businesses are highest-converting leads. Zero ad cost. Builds sustainable pipeline.

**Target Partners:**
| Partner Type | Referral Scenario | Approach |
|-------------|-------------------|----------|
| Real estate agents | Pre-sale inspections, buyer upgrades | Offer priority scheduling for their clients |
| Home inspectors | Panel replacements, wiring repairs flagged | Provide reliable referral partner experience |
| General contractors | Basement finishes, renovation electrical | Sub-contractor relationship |
| HVAC companies | Electrical for AC/furnace, circuit additions | Reciprocal referral agreement |
| Solar installers | Panel upgrades required for solar | Partnership for pre-solar electrical work |

**KPIs:**
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Active partnerships | 5-10 |
| Referral leads/month | 3-5 |
| Referral conversion rate | 50-70% |

---

## Supporting Activities (10% of effort)

### Website / SEO
- Optimize landing page for local keywords
- Add service-specific content (EV charger page, panel upgrade page)
- Blog content 2x/month for long-tail search
- Local SEO: citations, NAP consistency, schema markup

### Review Management
- Request review after every completed job (text or email)
- Respond to all reviews within 24 hours
- Target: 4-6 new Google reviews per month
- Monitor HomeAdvisor, Yelp, BBB

### Google Business Profile
- Complete all sections (services, hours, photos, description)
- Post weekly updates (offers, tips, project photos)
- Respond to Q&A and messages promptly
- Add new photos monthly (job sites, team, results)

## Implementation Priority

### Month 1: Foundation
1. Optimize Google Business Profile (free, highest impact)
2. Launch Google Local Services Ads
3. Claim Nextdoor business page
4. Set up email marketing platform
5. Create Facebook and Instagram business pages

### Month 2: Scale
1. Launch Google Search Ads (4 campaigns)
2. Begin Nextdoor organic posting cadence
3. Start Facebook/Instagram content calendar
4. Send first email newsletter
5. Begin local partnership outreach

### Month 3: Optimize
1. Analyze cost per lead by channel
2. Shift budget toward highest performers
3. Scale winning ad campaigns
4. Formalize 3-5 partnership agreements
5. Review and refine content strategy

### Quarterly: Review & Adjust
- Full channel performance review
- Budget reallocation based on ROI
- New channel testing (YouTube, TikTok for before/after)
- Partnership pipeline expansion
