## Messaging by Audience

### Homeowners (35-55, Family-Focused) — Persona 1

**Lead with:** Safety, reliability, protecting your family and home investment
**Key message:** "Your family's safety is our mission."

**Proof points to emphasize:**
- 4.9-star rating (social proof)
- Veteran-owned (trust signal)
- Comprehensive services (convenience)
- Free estimates (low risk)

**Objection handlers:**
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "You're a new company" | "Veteran discipline means we do it right from day one. Our 4.9-star rating proves it." |
| "Price concerns" | "Quality electrical work saves you money long-term. We provide honest, upfront estimates." |
| "How do I know you're legit?" | "Licensed, insured, veteran-owned, 4.9 stars on HomeAdvisor. We stake our name on every job." |

---

### EV / Tech-Forward Homeowners — Persona 2

**Lead with:** Modern expertise, future-proofing, convenience
**Key message:** "Your home, upgraded for the future."

**Proof points to emphasize:**
- EV charger expertise (specific service)
- Smart home integration (modern capability)
- Panel upgrades for growing power demands
- Permits handled (full service)

**Objection handlers:**
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Do you know EV chargers?" | "EV charger installation is one of our core services. We handle load calculations, permits, and installation." |
| "Can I DIY this?" | "A 240V circuit requires a licensed electrician and a permit. We handle it safely and to code." |
| "How long will it take?" | "Most EV charger installations take 4-6 hours. We schedule around your availability." |

---

### Aging Home Maintainers — Persona 3

**Lead with:** Trustworthiness, honest pricing, respect
**Key message:** "Honest service from an electrician you can trust."

**Proof points to emphasize:**
- Veteran-owned (honor and integrity)
- Honest pricing (no surprise charges)
- Clear explanations (no jargon)
- Respectful service (clean, careful work)

**Objection handlers:**
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "I've been burned by contractors" | "Glen is a veteran — honesty and integrity aren't just words, they're values we live by." |
| "Is this really necessary?" | "We'll explain exactly what we find and why it matters. You decide — no pressure." |
| "That sounds expensive" | "We provide a written estimate before any work begins. No surprises, no hidden fees." |
