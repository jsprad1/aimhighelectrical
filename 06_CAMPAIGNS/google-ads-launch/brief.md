## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| High CPC in competitive market | Start with long-tail keywords, optimize Quality Score |
| Low conversion rate | A/B test landing page, add contact form (done), optimize CTAs |
| Budget exhaustion early in day | Set ad scheduling to peak hours (7am-7pm) |
| Irrelevant clicks | Robust negative keyword list, geographic restrictions |
| No call tracking | Implement CallRail before launch for attribution |

---

## Success Criteria (3-Month Review)

| Metric | Pass | Fail |
|--------|------|------|

| Leads per Month | 5+ | <3 |
