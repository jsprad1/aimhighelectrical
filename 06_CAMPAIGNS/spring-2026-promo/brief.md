**Target Services:**
- Whole-home generator installation

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| Low response to free inspection offer | Medium | Medium | Emphasize $149 value, add urgency ("limited availability"), retarget with panel upgrade offer |
| April tax refund timing mismatch | Low | Low | Extend April offer into early May if needed |
| Severe weather delays generator installs | Medium | Medium | Build scheduling buffer, communicate proactively with customers |
| Google Ads budget exhaustion | Medium | Medium | Set daily budget caps, pause low-performing keywords, focus on high-intent terms |
| Direct mail low response rate | Medium | Low | Supplement with digital retargeting, strong QR code CTA |
| Competing electrician promotions | High | Medium | Emphasize veteran-owned + 4.9-star differentiation, offer superior value |
| Lead quality issues (tire-kickers) | Medium | Medium | Qualify leads with questions, focus on service-specific keywords vs. general |
| Glen's capacity constraints | Low | High | Schedule jobs strategically, communicate lead times clearly, consider subcontractor backup |
