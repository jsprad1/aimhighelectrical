## Review Generation Strategy

### Goal
Reach 50+ Google reviews within 6 months (4.8+ average rating).

### When to Ask
- Immediately after job completion (same day)
- Follow up 24-48 hours later via text/email if no response
- Never ask during an active complaint or issue

### How to Ask
1. **In-person (best conversion):** "It was great working on your home today. If you're happy with the work, a Google review would mean the world to our small business. I can text you the link."
2. **Text message (24 hrs later):** "Hi [Name], thanks for choosing Aim High! If you have a minute, a Google review helps other homeowners find us: [link]. Thank you! — Glen"
3. **Email follow-up (48 hrs):** Include in post-service follow-up email with direct review link

### Review Link
Create short URL: `g.page/aimhighelectrical/review` (set up after GBP is verified)

### Response Templates

**Positive Review Response:**
> "Thank you [Name]! We really appreciate you taking the time to share your experience. It's our mission to deliver reliable, quality electrical work for every Aurora homeowner. We're here whenever you need us. — Glen"

**Negative Review Response:**
> "[Name], thank you for your feedback. We take every concern seriously and want to make this right. Please call me directly at 720-326-8492 so we can discuss your experience. We stand behind our work and your satisfaction matters to us. — Glen"
