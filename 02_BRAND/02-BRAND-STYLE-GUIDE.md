## Content Guidelines

### Headlines
- Lead with benefits
- Use action verbs
- Keep under 10 words

### Body Copy
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Bullet points for features
- Simple language

### CTAs
- Action-oriented verbs
- Specific about next step
- Create value without pressure

---

## Accessibility

### Color Contrast
- All text must meet WCAG AA standards (4.5:1 minimum)

### Best Practices
- Alt text for all images
- Keyboard navigable
- Font size minimum 16px
- Touch targets minimum 44x44px
